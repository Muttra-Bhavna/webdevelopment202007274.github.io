The Ideal Place For Your Sweet Tooth




The heritage of a family line of chocolate makers, Treat Time, was created in 2005 by Sara, who wanted to start her own business after arriving in Mauritius. She started by making chocolates as she has always known from her family and had a booming business here on the island.

However, being fond of learning, Sara decided to try making candies, whereby she would diversify her products and being motivated, gave her the perfect results she wanted. As such, time went by and, she started practising the making of many more products making her business boom more and more since her customers loved her products.

With the passing years, she was also able to create more job opportunities since her booming business needed more people to work.

Now Treat Time is a shop where one can find all kinds of chocolate, candies and sweets pastries for your loved ones; together with a range of refreshments like coffee, tea, milkshake, fresh fruit juice, among others; according to your taste.

Treat Time also provides the service of cake making for several occasions like birthdays, weddings, receptions, amongst others
